# PopMyVideo — facts for blog posts

Only claim what's listed here.

## What it is

Mobile app (Android and iOS) that analyzes video footage and layers animated text, icons, shapes, and visual effects on top — timed to the action. Your original footage stays intact; polish is added as overlays, not by regenerating the video.

Built by ThinkingCats (https://thinkingcats.com).

**Positioning:** Your clip is done filming. It's not done posting. One-clip polish layer — import one clip, export a finished Reel, Short, or ad.

## What it does

- Import a clip from your phone — any length, with or without sound
- Reads visuals and listens when there's audio; finds moments worth enhancing
- Prompt the vibe: product ad, hype hook, clean label, celebration burst
- Preview moving text, icons, and effects synced to your footage
- Export a polished MP4 to Photos or share to social
- Optional sound effects — impacts, whooshes, stings (great for silent clips)
- Trim, reframe, and grade before export

## Use cases

1. **Silent B-roll → ad** — product shots without audio get text overlays, motion graphics, and SFX
2. **Podcast clip → Short** — animated captions and emphasis graphics on clips you've already cut (not clip finding)
3. **Talking-head → educational** — stat cards, checkmarks, arrows on key claims; captions synced to speech
4. **Reels & TikTok** — one usable take polished with captions and effects, no timeline

## What it does NOT do

- Regenerate or restyle your original footage (overlays only)
- Static stickers — graphics are animated and timed to the action
- Full multi-track timeline editing (assembly cuts, multi-track workflows)
- Podcast clipper — import a clip you've already cut; we add the polish
- Desktop / web editor

## What it's not (trust lines)

- Not AI video generation — your pixels stay yours
- Not static stickers — everything is animated and timed
- Not a full editor — no timeline maze
- Not a podcast clipper — bring the clip you've already cut; we add the polish

## Privacy

- AI generates animation **code** (effect specs) from compressed frames — not a new video file
- Preview and export **composite effects on your phone**; full video is never cloud re-encoded
- Compressed frames/audio sent for codegen are **deleted as soon as the job finishes**
- No project cloud sync; projects stay on device
- Account email and subscription state kept while account is active only

See https://popmyvideo.com/privacy/ for full policy.
